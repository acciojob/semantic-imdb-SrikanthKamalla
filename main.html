Summary

"Inception" is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. It explores the concept of dream invasion and extraction. Dom Cobb, a skilled thief, is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can perform inception—planting an idea in someone's mind through dreams.
