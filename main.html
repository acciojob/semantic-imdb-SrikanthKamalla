Summary

"Inception" is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets by infiltrating the subconscious during dreams. He is offered redemption if he can succeed in the impossible task of inception—planting an idea in a target’s mind.
